What are we doing?

C<>DEbreak is a platform for CODE students to organise their lunch breaks – we like efficiency after all. Find all of our available options below!

Pre Order

With the pre-order option you can order your lunch a day before so you can have a simplified lunchtime. From the preselected restaurant of the day you can pick dark meat, light meat, or veggie.

Head Out

Organize a group of CODE students to go out and eat together – with none of the issues of asking around to find out who wants to join and when to go. You’ll take care of all that ahead of time.

Take away

Order food together and bring it back to campus! You can create a notification to let other CODE students know that you’ll be heading out to a restaurant to grab some food. You’ll bring it all back, they’ll pay you, and that’s it!
